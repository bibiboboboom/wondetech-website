Core Capabilities

Built for enterprise knowledge at scale

Knowledge Hub applies governance, search, tracing, and version control to all your knowledge assets — not just documents.

AI Chat & Intelligent Search

Ask questions in natural language. Answers are generated from your verified enterprise knowledge — not the internet. Works like an internal enterprise AI assistant, grounded in your own documents, procedures, records, and expert knowledge. Every answer is traceable to its source.

Source Tracing

Every answer links back to its exact source — document title, version number, section, expert session, or training record. Not just "here's an answer" — but "here's where it comes from." Your teams can cite, trust, and act on knowledge with confidence.

Version & Change History

All knowledge assets are version-controlled with full change tracking. When a procedure updates, every connected application knows.

Governance & Access Control

Role-based access controls and audit logs ensure the right people see the right knowledge — with complete records of what was accessed and when.

Multi-Source Ingestion

Documents, spreadsheets, transcripts, Q&A records, operational logs — Knowledge Hub ingests them all without requiring reformatting.

Closed-Loop Knowledge Capture

Training results, employee feedback, and updated field records flow back into the knowledge base — enriching it with every use.

Enterprise-Scale Ingestion

Built for large, complex files

Building an enterprise knowledge base is more than uploading scanned documents. Oilfield, engineering, government, and manufacturing organizations commonly handle oversized files — high-resolution scans, technical drawings, PDF archives, and legacy records that can reach gigabytes in size. Generic tools cannot parse, index, or search them. WondeTech can.

Large File Splitting
Oversized files are intelligently split into processable segments without losing context or structure
OCR & Content Extraction
Scanned documents, technical drawings, and image-based PDFs are converted to searchable, structured text
Metadata Extraction
Document type, version, author, date, topic, and classification tags are automatically extracted and indexed
Multi-Stage Processing
Segments are enriched, cross-referenced, and validated across multiple processing passes for accuracy
Knowledge Structuring
Segments are recombined with relationships, hierarchies, and cross-references preserved in the knowledge graph
AI-Ready Knowledge Base
Fully searchable, AI chat-ready, and source-traceable — ready for intelligent Q&A and learning applications
From a single multi-gigabyte technical archive to a fully searchable, AI-ready knowledge asset — without manual reformatting or data loss.
