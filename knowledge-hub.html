Core Capabilities

Built for enterprise knowledge at scale

Knowledge Hub applies governance, search, tracing, and version control to all your knowledge assets — not just documents.

🔍

Intelligent Search

Full-text and semantic search across all knowledge assets — documents, records, transcripts, expert Q&A. Get relevant answers in seconds.

🔗

Source Tracing

Every answer links back to its source — document, version, section, expert session, or training output. Your teams always know where knowledge comes from.

🔄

Version & Change History

All knowledge assets are version-controlled with full change tracking. When a procedure updates, every connected application knows.

🏛️

Governance & Access Control

Role-based access controls and audit logs ensure the right people see the right knowledge — with complete records of what was accessed and when.

📥

Multi-Source Ingestion

Documents, spreadsheets, transcripts, Q&A records, operational logs — Knowledge Hub ingests them all without requiring reformatting.

🔄

Closed-Loop Knowledge Capture

Training results, employee feedback, and updated field records flow back into the knowledge base — enriching it with every use.

How It Works

From scattered assets to enterprise knowledge foundation

A four-step process that turns diverse knowledge sources into a governed, growing knowledge base.

01

Collect

Connect your document repositories, training systems, expert Q&A channels, and operational records. Knowledge Hub ingests multiple source types in their original formats.

→
02

Structure

Knowledge assets are parsed, categorized, and indexed. Sources, versions, and relationships are captured — making implicit knowledge explicit and searchable.

→
03

Govern

Access controls, version tracking, and audit logging are applied automatically. Your governance policies define who can read, search, and update each asset.

→
04

Apply & Enrich

Learning Hub and Contract Review draw from the same knowledge base. Training results and field feedback flow back, continuously enriching your enterprise knowledge.
