Why Contract Review

Contract review that actually uses what you know

Traditional contract review is slow, inconsistent, and disconnected from your operational standards. Contract Review changes the benchmark from generic to yours.

⚡

Automated Clause Extraction

Every clause is extracted, categorized, and reviewed against your policy library automatically — reducing review time from days to hours.

🚨

Risk Flagging

High-risk clauses are surfaced immediately — liability limits, payment terms, force majeure, termination rights. Nothing hides in the fine print.

🔗

Evidence-Traced Findings

Every flagged clause links to the specific policy, standard, or precedent that triggered the finding. Your team knows exactly what to push back on — and why.

Product in Action

What a contract review looks like

Contract — Drilling Services Agreement (MSA-2026-04)
● High Risk
"Section 8.3 — Limitation of Liability: Contractor's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the three (3) months preceding the claim, regardless of the nature of the claim..."
● Review Required
"Section 11.2 — Payment Terms: All invoices shall be paid within ninety (90) days of submission, unless disputed in writing within fifteen (15) days..."
✓ Clear
"Section 4.1 — HSE Requirements: Contractor shall comply with all applicable health, safety, and environmental laws and regulations in force at the location of work..."
● High Risk
"Section 14.4 — Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Contractor's home jurisdiction..."
Risk Summary
2 High Risk
3 Review
8 Clear
High Risk §8.3 — Limitation of Liability
Liability cap of 3 months' fees is significantly below standard. Your MSA template requires minimum 12 months' contract value.
🔗 Sourced from: Procurement Policy PP-004 §3.1 — Minimum Liability Standards
Recommended: Negotiate to 12-month minimum or flag for legal sign-off
Review §11.2 — Payment Terms
Net 90 payment terms exceed your standard Net 30 policy. Requires Finance approval for exception.
🔗 Sourced from: Finance Policy FP-012 §2.4 — Standard Payment Terms
Recommended: Escalate to Finance for exception approval
High Risk §14.4 — Governing Law
Governing law set to Contractor's jurisdiction. Your policy requires disputes to be governed under local jurisdiction.
🔗 Sourced from: Legal Framework LF-002 §7 — Dispute Resolution Requirements
Recommended: Require governing law amendment before execution
