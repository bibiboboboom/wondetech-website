Case 01

Majnoon

Oilfield Information Management & Technical Knowledge Reuse

Knowledge Hub Learning Hub
Client Snapshot
  • Client
    Majnoon
  • Industry
    Oil & Gas
  • Region
    Iraq
  • Scenario
    Unifying engineering documentation, field records, and expert knowledge for oilfield operations
Key Outcome

Engineering documents, field records, and operational knowledge were unified into a single searchable platform — reducing document search time and making field expertise reusable across teams.

Challenge
  • Engineering, procurement, and operational documents were stored in separate repositories
  • Teams spent too much time searching for the correct version of technical references
  • Tacit knowledge from experienced field staff was not captured, structured, or reusable
What WondeTech Changed
  • Unified engineering files, field records, procedures, and expert Q&A into one governed knowledge base
  • Added searchable tags, source traceability, and version control for key knowledge assets
  • Structured field experience and expert answers into reusable operational knowledge
Business Impact
Faster document retrieval
Engineers find technical references in seconds instead of hours
Unified engineering knowledge base
One source of truth for all engineering, procurement, and operational documents
Expert knowledge preserved
Field expertise captured and made searchable and reusable
Lower project risk
Teams rely on verified, version-controlled operational knowledge
Module Contribution
Knowledge Hub
Unifies engineering files, field records, procedures, and expert knowledge into a searchable, governed, and traceable knowledge base.
Learning Hub
Uses selected verified knowledge assets as downstream training material when needed.
Case 02

BOC

Role-Based Learning & Workforce Capability Development

Learning Hub Knowledge Hub
Client Snapshot
  • Client
    BOC
  • Industry
    Oil & Gas
  • Region
    Iraq
  • Scenario
    Learning Hub for local workforce onboarding, role readiness, and EDP-driven capability development
Key Outcome

Operational knowledge and training materials were transformed into structured learning paths — helping employees onboard faster, close competency gaps, and progress toward role readiness.

Challenge
  • New employees lacked a clear role-based learning path
  • Training materials and operational knowledge were available but not connected to competency goals
  • Managers had limited visibility into learning progress, skill gaps, and readiness by role
What WondeTech Changed
  • Built role-based onboarding and learning paths using Knowledge Hub as the content foundation
  • Connected learning plans with EDP, job requirements, and competency models
  • Captured learning progress, feedback, and assessment results back into the knowledge loop
Business Impact
Faster employee onboarding
New hires follow structured learning paths from day one
Clearer role readiness
Employees progress along defined competency milestones per role
Targeted competency gap closure
EDP-driven development plans address specific skill gaps by role
Continuous learning feedback loop
Assessment results and field insights flow back into knowledge assets
Module Contribution
Knowledge Hub

Learning Hub
Turns enterprise knowledge into onboarding paths, role-based learning maps, EDP development plans, and measurable competency growth.

Data Security Knowledge Management & Delivery Enablement

Knowledge Hub Learning Hub

  • Client

  • Industry

  • Region

  • Scenario
    Knowledge Hub and Learning Hub for data security governance, delivery process standardization, and project team enablement
Key Outcome

Data security rules, delivery documents, SOPs, and customer project experience were structured into reusable knowledge assets — helping project teams understand security requirements faster and deliver work more consistently.

Challenge
  • Data security requirements, delivery SOPs, and customer-specific project knowledge were scattered across documents and team experience

  • Created searchable tags and source traceability for key security and delivery knowledge
  • Used Learning Hub to support onboarding, data security training, and project delivery enablement for new team members
Business Impact

Faster project onboarding

More consistent delivery quality

Case 04

U.S. Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Company

Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Knowledge & Compliance Training

Knowledge Hub Learning Hub
Client Snapshot
  • Client
    U.S. Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Company
  • Industry
    Pharmaceutical Manufacturing
  • Region
    United States
  • Scenario
    Knowledge Hub and Learning Hub for pharmaceutical SOP management, production knowledge reuse, and compliance training
Key Outcome

Pharmaceutical SOPs, quality standards, equipment documents, batch records, production issue cases, and training materials were organized into a structured knowledge foundation — helping employees learn procedures faster and follow compliance requirements more consistently.

Challenge
  • SOPs, quality documents, equipment manuals, batch records, and production issue cases were stored across different systems and folders
  • Production and quality teams needed quick access to the latest procedures, quality standards, and compliance requirements
  • New employees required repeated manual training on safety, compliance, equipment operation, and production workflows
What WondeTech Changed
  • Built a Knowledge Hub for SOPs, quality standards, equipment manuals, batch records, production issue cases, and compliance materials
  • Added version control, tags, and source traceability to make operational and quality knowledge easier to find and verify
  • Used Learning Hub to create role-based onboarding, compliance training, and production skill development paths
Business Impact
Faster production onboarding
New employees learn SOPs, safety rules, and operating procedures through structured paths
Better compliance readiness
Training content is connected with verified SOPs, quality standards, and audit-ready records
Reusable production knowledge
Recurring issues, equipment experience, and process improvements become reusable knowledge assets
More consistent operations
Teams work from the same updated procedures, quality standards, and compliance requirements
