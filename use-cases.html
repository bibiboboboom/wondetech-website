Case 02
🛢️ Majnoon
Oilfield Information Management & Evidence-Based Contract Review
Knowledge Hub Contract Review
Key Outcome

Contract review supported by direct access to engineering docs, approval records, acceptance materials, and project files — no more manual attachment chasing.

Connecting contract documents with engineering records, approval files, and field delivery evidence

📂
The Situation

Engineering, procurement, contract, approval, acceptance, and delivery documents were stored separately. When reviewers needed to check a contract claim or verify a deliverable, they spent significant time locating the relevant attachments, confirming version history, and tracing approval chains.

⚡
What Changed

WondeTech connected contract text with the associated technical attachments, approval records, acceptance materials, and project files stored in Knowledge Hub. Reviewers can now navigate from a contract clause directly to the supporting document — and see which version was current at signing.

📈
Business Value

Review time reduced by eliminating manual attachment searches. Approval trail is clear and auditable. Disputes and claims can be resolved with direct evidence — reducing negotiation time and project risk.

Cross-Border Contract Drafting & Review Workflow
Contract Review Knowledge Hub
Key Outcome

Historical agreements, standard templates, and risk clauses consolidated into a single workflow — from first-draft preparation through to final sign-off.

Reusing institutional knowledge in cross-border contract drafting and review

📂
The Situation

The team handled English-language contracts, service agreements, cooperation terms, and compliance documents. Relevant precedents — historical agreements, standard templates, risk clauses, and internal review notes — existed but were not easily reachable when drafting or reviewing a new agreement.

Case 04
🧪 U.S. Chemical-Pharma Startup
Lean Team Compliance & Risk Prioritization
Contract Review Knowledge Hub
Key Outcome

Small team can screen key contract risks before formal legal review — prioritizing high-risk documents and focusing on business decisions that matter.

Helping a small team screen contract risk and prioritize what needs legal attention

📂
The Situation

A lean team was responsible for R&D collaboration agreements, supplier contracts, NDAs, IP documents, and customer contracts — without the bandwidth to review every document thoroughly before sending to legal. Important risks were sometimes missed at the business stage, only surfacing late in the process.

⚡
What Changed

WondeTech screens incoming contracts against the team's own benchmarks — internal policies, approved templates, and IP protection standards stored in Knowledge Hub. The system flags high-risk terms and surfaces relevant precedents, so the business team can triage documents before legal review rather than after.

📈
Business Value

The team focuses legal time on the contracts that genuinely need it. Business decisions are better informed — the team understands the key risks before committing to terms. Compliance requirements are checked systematically rather than depending on individual reviewers to remember every rule.

Contract Review
Risk screening calibrated to your own compliance benchmarks and IP requirements
🏛️
Knowledge Hub
Internal policies, approved templates, and IP standards used as the review benchmark
