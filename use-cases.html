Case 01

BOC

Enterprise Knowledge Base & Workforce Enablement

Knowledge Hub Learning Hub
Client Snapshot
  • Client
    BOC
  • Industry
    Oil & Gas
  • Region
    Iraq
  • Scenario
    Enterprise knowledge base and workforce enablement
Key Outcome

Scattered technical documents and training records structured into a governed knowledge base — enabling faster onboarding and role-based capability development across field sites.

Challenge
  • Technical, training, and IP documents scattered across teams with no unified search
  • Employees repeatedly asked colleagues to locate the right version of procedures
  • No structured onboarding path — new hires had to piece together relevant knowledge themselves
What WondeTech Changed
  • Built a governed knowledge base for all document types with full-text search
  • Made content version-controlled and accessible across all field site teams
  • Connected the knowledge base to role-based learning paths for structured onboarding
Business Impact
Faster employee onboarding
New hires access structured, role-relevant materials directly
Less document searching
One search covers all teams and document types
Role-based development paths
Learning paths tied to enterprise-specific operational standards
Better knowledge control
IP-sensitive content governed with role-based access controls

Case 02

Majnoon

Oilfield Information Management & Technical Knowledge Reuse

Knowledge Hub Learning Hub
Client Snapshot
  • Client
    Majnoon
  • Industry
    Oil & Gas
  • Region
    Iraq
  • Scenario
    Unifying engineering documentation and field knowledge for operations and onboarding
Key Outcome

Engineering documents, field records, and operational knowledge unified in a single searchable platform — cutting document search time and accelerating new engineer onboarding.

Challenge
  • Engineering, procurement, and operational documents stored in separate repositories
  • New engineers spent weeks searching for the right technical references
  • Tacit knowledge from experienced field staff was not captured or reusable
What WondeTech Changed
  • Unified engineering files, field records, and expert Q&A into a single searchable knowledge base
  • Captured expert knowledge from field sessions and made it available for training
  • Built role-based onboarding paths for new field engineers using the unified knowledge base
Business Impact
Faster document retrieval
Engineers find technical references in seconds instead of hours
Faster new engineer onboarding
Structured role-based paths using real field knowledge
Expert knowledge preserved
Field expertise captured and made searchable and reusable
Lower project risk
Teams rely on verified, version-controlled operational knowledge
