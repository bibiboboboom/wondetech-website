Contract Review

Faster reviews. Fewer surprises. Every clause traced to policy.

Contract Review applies your operational knowledge and compliance standards to automated contract analysis. Risk clauses are flagged against your own policy library. Every finding is traceable to the specific document or rule that triggered it.

  • Automated clause extraction and risk flagging
  • Comparison against your own policy and regulatory library
  • Evidence tracing: every flag links to source knowledge
  • Structured review reports for legal and operations teams
