Foundation
01

Knowledge Hub

Your enterprise knowledge asset foundation.

Knowledge Hub structures documents, operational records, training outputs, expert sessions, and field experience into a governed, searchable, traceable knowledge base. It is the foundation that powers every other WondeTech product.

  • Multi-source ingestion: documents, records, transcripts, Q&A, and field reports
  • Full-text and semantic search with source tracing
  • Version control and change history for all knowledge assets
  • Role-based governance, access control, and audit logging
