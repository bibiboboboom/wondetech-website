03

Contract Review

Faster reviews. Fewer surprises. Every clause traced to policy.

Contract Review applies your own policies, templates, and past cases to automated contract analysis. Clauses are flagged against your enterprise knowledge base — not generic benchmarks.

  • Automated clause extraction and risk categorization
  • Comparison against your own policy library and past cases
  • Evidence tracing: every flag links to source policy or precedent
  • Structured review reports for legal and operations teams
