01

Knowledge Hub

Your enterprise knowledge asset foundation.

Knowledge Hub structures documents, operational records, training outputs, expert sessions, and field experience into a governed, searchable, traceable knowledge base. It is the foundation that powers Learning Hub and daily knowledge-driven operations.

  • Multi-source ingestion: documents, records, transcripts, Q&A, and field reports
  • Full-text and semantic search with source tracing
  • Version control and change history for all knowledge assets
  • Role-based governance, access control, and audit logging

02

Learning Hub

Localized workforce enablement — powered by your own knowledge.

Learning Hub uses your enterprise-specific and industry-specific knowledge base to build role-based learning paths, identify competency gaps, and close them with targeted development. Every training result feeds back into Knowledge Hub.

  • Role-based competency maps tied to enterprise-specific standards
  • EDP-based development plans with gap closure tracking
  • Learning content sourced and versioned from Knowledge Hub
  • Progress tracking and readiness dashboards for managers

03

Business Content Intelligence

Apply enterprise knowledge to business content — risk review, source tracing, human confirmation.

Business Content Intelligence applies your governed knowledge base to business content workflows — starting with sales contracts and commercial documents. It helps teams identify risks earlier across sales, product, marketing, HR, operations, and legal documents. Every risk check connects back to your own standards, policies, and historical cases in Knowledge Hub.

  • Risk detection across product, sales, marketing, contract, and operational content
  • Knowledge Hub matching: standards, regulations, templates, historical issues
  • Source-traceable risk explanations linked to governing knowledge
  • Closed-loop learning: reviewed risks feed back into Knowledge Hub
