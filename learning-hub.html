Why Learning Hub

🗺️

Role-Based Learning Maps

📊

Competency Gap Analysis

🎯

Targeted Growth Actions

Rather than assigning a generic course catalog, Learning Hub surfaces specific learning actions for specific gaps — reducing time-to-readiness dramatically.

📈

Manager Dashboards

How It Works

From documents to measurable readiness

🏛️
Step 1

Knowledge Foundation

Learning Hub reads from Knowledge Hub to understand your operational standards, procedures, and requirements for each role.

↓
🗺️
Step 2

Competency Mapping

Each role is mapped to the knowledge and skills it requires. Maps are structured, not improvised — derived directly from your own documentation.

↓
🔍
Step 3

Gap Identification

Each employee's current knowledge state is assessed against their role map. Gaps are scored by severity so the most critical learning needs surface first.

↓
🎓
Step 4

Targeted Learning

Employees receive focused learning assignments tied to their specific gaps — not generic courses. Content comes directly from the knowledge base.

↓
✅
Step 5

Verified Readiness

Role readiness scores update as employees complete learning. Managers and HR see real-time readiness across teams — with full audit trails.
